03 Why this exists

Built for freshers, by a fresher.

The fresher job hunt in India is genuinely broken. You bounce between LinkedIn, Naukri, Workday portals, company career pages — and you still miss Google apprenticeships because they only post for 48 hours.

This site pulls from ~25 sources daily and filters out the noise — no BPO walk-ins, no "Customer Service Representative" mass hiring, no recruiter spam. Just the tier-1 MNC roles freshers actually want.

Open source Free forever No login No tracking
